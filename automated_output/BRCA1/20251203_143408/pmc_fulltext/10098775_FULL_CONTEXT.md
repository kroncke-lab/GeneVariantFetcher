## Prevalence of BRCA1 and BRCA2 Jewish mutations in Spanish breast cancer patients

### Abstract

We screened the 185delAG and 5382insC (BRCA1) and the 6174delT (BRCA2) mutation in 298 Spanish women with breast cancer. Two women (one with Sephardic ancestors) presented the 185delAG mutation and the same haplotype reported in Ashkenazim with this mutation.
